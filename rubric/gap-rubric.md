# Gap-scoring rubric

Score each profile 0/1 on the following, using the merged profile + post
dataset. A profile with several 0s is a strong candidate; a profile with
mostly 1s is a weak fit regardless of seniority.

1. **Personal narrative** — does the person talk about their own journey
   (career path, lessons learned, turning points), or only about their
   company/product?
2. **Positioning / POV** — is there a clear, differentiated point of view,
   or does the profile read as generic and interchangeable with peers?
3. **Engagement activity** — are they commenting on others' posts, or
   inactive in the community layer entirely?
4. **Posting consistency** — regular cadence, sporadic, or dormant?
5. **Content quality** — substantive and specific, or surface-level and
   vague?
